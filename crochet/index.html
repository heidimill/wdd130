Welcome to the Crochet Catalogue!

Crochet is a great way to practice meditation while activating the creative part of your brain. A further benefit is the tangible product created by your own hands.

Relaxing

I personally have just started crocheting. I find it so relaxing and calming. The repetitive motion of my hands gives me something to focus on without having to worry about stressful life problems.

Productive

This act of crocheting is a hobby surrounded by peace and vibrancy in the various results. Having a tangible physical result gives the brain satisfaction. Plus there is the added bonus of having a skill that translates into making home-made gifts for friends, family, and anyone who needs it.
